Characters

Gon Freecss

Gon Freecss is a young boy determined to become a Hunter to find his father, Ging Freecss, who is a legendary Hunter.

Killua Zoldyck

Killua Zoldyck is Gon’s best friend and a member of the famous assassin family, the Zoldycks. He is a skilled assassin with a strong sense of loyalty.

Hisoka

Hisoka Morow, known by various aliases such as Hisoka the Magician and Grim Reaper, plays a prominent role as an antagonist in the Hunter x Hunter anime/manga series. Previously affiliated with the Phantom Troupe, he possesses both magical abilities and a penchant for serial killing.

Episodes

Episode 131 Anger × And × Light

Episode 131 is a monumental episode as it officially kicks off the fight between Gon and Neferpitou. Finding out that he's been lied to, and Kite is really dead, Gon loses his last shred of sanity and hope and forces his body to transform his body to an age where he's strong enough to completely, and utterly kill Neferpitou.

Episode 119 Strong × Or × Weak

In this episode, Gon, Killua, and their allies continue their battle against the Chimera Ants. Specifically, this episode focuses on the confrontation between Knuckle Bine and Shoot McMahon against the Chimera Ants Youpi and Yupi. It's a pivotal moment in the Chimera Ant arc where the strength and weaknesses of both sides are showcased, hence the title "Strong × Or × Weak."

Episode 15 Explosion × Of × Deception

Gon and Killua participate in the Hunter Exam's fourth phase, which involves a series of one-on-one battles. The episode likely features intense combat, strategy, and perhaps some unexpected twists or deceptions, as suggested by the title. Each phase of the Hunter Exam tests the candidates' abilities and resourcefulness, and this episode is likely to showcase those traits as Gon and Killua face off against their opponents.
